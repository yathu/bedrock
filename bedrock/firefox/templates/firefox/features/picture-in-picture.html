Cool feature: Picture-in-Picture

Get more done with pop-out videos

Got things to do and things to watch? Do both using Picture-in-Picture in Firefox. It lets you pop a video out of its webpage and pin it to your screen so you can keep watching while you’re on other pages, tabs and apps.

Save yourself time by multitasking with Picture-in-Picture, built right inside the Firefox web browser.

{{ download_firefox(dom_id='features-pip-download-primary', download_location='primary cta') }}

Here’s how it works:

  1. Play any video in your Firefox browser, like this one starring red panda cubs.
  2. Hover your cursor over the video and click the Picture-in-Picture button . This pops out the video.
  3. Cruise around to other tabs or even outside of Firefox. The video stays put!

3 ways to use Picture-in-Picture

Watch a lecture or meeting while you take notes

Keep a tutorial video open with a recipe while you cook

Entertain cats, dogs and kids while you get work done
